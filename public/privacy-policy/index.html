The public site should only collect what is necessary to run the site, respond to inquiries, and support legitimate business operations.

What AI Syndicate Does Not Intentionally Collect

The site is not designed to collect sensitive or consumer-finance data through the public marketing pages.

No online account system

The site does not currently provide customer logins, member accounts, or consumer self-service profiles.

No payment collection on the public site

The public website does not currently process online payments, card data, or checkout information.

No advertising pixels in the public code

The current public site code does not intentionally deploy Meta Pixel, Google Ads tags, or other advertising trackers for cross-site behavioral targeting.

No sensitive information requested

Please do not send Social Security numbers, financial account details, government-issued identifiers, protected health information, or confidential third-party data through the public website unless a secure channel and clear business need have been established.

Use and Disclosure

How AI Syndicate may use and disclose personal information.

Business purposes

  • Responding to business inquiries.
  • Reviewing project fit and preparing proposals.
  • Delivering strategy, advisory, or implementation services.
  • Maintaining internal business records.
  • Protecting the site, investigating abuse, and troubleshooting errors.
  • Complying with contractual, legal, tax, or recordkeeping obligations.

Categories of recipients

  • Hosting, CDN, infrastructure, and security providers that help deliver and protect the site.
  • Email, productivity, or communications providers used to correspond about inquiries or active work.
  • Professional advisors such as lawyers, accountants, or consultants where reasonably necessary.
  • Regulators, law enforcement, courts, or counterparties when disclosure is legally required or reasonably necessary to protect rights.
  • A successor entity in a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or asset transfer, subject to applicable law.

AI Syndicate does not sell personal information and does not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising as those concepts are commonly used in California privacy law.

Access, Correction, Deletion, and Questions

How to request changes or ask privacy questions.

The site does not provide a self-service account portal. If you want to review, correct, or request deletion of the personal information you submitted directly to AI Syndicate, contact cj@aisyndicate.com with enough information for the business to understand and verify the request.

Request method

Email cj@aisyndicate.com with the subject line that best matches your request, such as “Privacy Request,” “Correction Request,” or “Deletion Request.”

Verification

AI Syndicate may need to verify your identity or authority before disclosing, correcting, or deleting records, and may decline or narrow a request where permitted by law.

Applicable law

Depending on your location and the nature of the relationship, applicable law may give you rights to request to know or access personal information, request correction, request deletion, or appeal a denied request. AI Syndicate will review requests in light of any applicable legal obligations, verification requirements, and available exceptions. Because the current public site is not intentionally configured to sell personal information or share it for cross-context behavioral advertising, there is not currently a separate sale/sharing opt-out flow on the site.

Questions or complaints

Questions about this policy or the site’s information practices may be sent to cj@aisyndicate.com.

CalOPPA And Online Disclosures

How the site handles online privacy issues specific to website use.

Material changes

If AI Syndicate makes material changes to this Privacy Policy, the revised policy will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Where appropriate, the site may also call attention to material changes through additional on-site notice.

Do Not Track

The current public website is not configured to change behavior in response to browser “Do Not Track” signals. The public codebase also is not intentionally configured for cross-site behavioral advertising or profile-building ad-tech.

Other parties collecting across sites

AI Syndicate does not intentionally permit advertising networks or social media trackers to collect information about your activity across different websites from the public site. Hosting and infrastructure providers may still receive ordinary technical request data needed to deliver and secure the site.

Children

The site is directed to businesses and adult users, not children. AI Syndicate does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through the public website.

Security and Limits

How AI Syndicate approaches security.

Reasonable safeguards

AI Syndicate seeks to use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures appropriate to the nature of the information it actually maintains.

No absolute guarantee

No website, email system, or online transmission method can guarantee absolute security. Please avoid sending highly sensitive information through the public site or open email unless a secure channel has been established.

Need a privacy answer tied to the site’s actual data practices?

If you have a privacy question, a request concerning personal information, or need confirmation about how the current site is configured, contact AI Syndicate directly.
